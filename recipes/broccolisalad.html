Broccoli Salad

Broccoli Salad is a quick salad full of fresh crisp broccoli, dried cranberries, sunflower seeds, and bacon bits. It’s all tossed in a really easy creamy homemade dressing.

Ingredients

8 cups broccoli cut into bite-sized pieces

⅓ cup red onion diced

½ cup dried cranberries

¼ cup sunflower seeds

½ cup bacon bits

Dressing

1 cup mayonnaise

3 tablespoons cider vinegar

2 tablespoons sugar

salt & pepper to taste

Directions

1. Whisk together dressing ingredients in a medium bowl. Set aside

2. Whisk together dressing ingredients in a medium bowl. Set aside

3. Refrigerate for at least one hour before serving.
